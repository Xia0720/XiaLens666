About Me

Hi, I’m Xia.

I’m a photography enthusiast from China, currently at the beginning of my journey behind the lens. I have a deep passion for capturing people, nature, and the beauty of fleeting moments. Through my photos, I aim to freeze time, preserve memories, and celebrate the simple joys of life.

This website is not only a collection of my favorite shots, but also a visual diary of my growth as a photographer. I hope my work brings you a sense of beauty and inspiration—let’s embrace the magic in every moment, together.
